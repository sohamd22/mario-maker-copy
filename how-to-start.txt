
Start off by signing up and logging in
(You can always delete your account).

If you don't want to sign in, you can 'Play As Guest',
the guest account has a premade playable world that cannot be 
updated or deleted.

Once you've logged in, you will be presented with a worlds
menu where you can create your own worlds by clicking
on the 'New World' button.

In the new world window, you can modify the world to your liking
and then save the world to make it playable.
Click the exit button after you have are happy with your world.
You can also update and delete the world after making it.

The world will now appear in the worlds menu where you can play it.
All the world statistics are saved so you can get new highscores.
